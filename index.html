Professional Profile

I am Elaulas Cristiano. Currently I live in Keller, Texas but I was born and raised in a small farm town in Kansas. Growing up in Kansas was a great experience, we worked for everything we had and simply put, life seemed much simpler. I consider myself fortunate to have grown up in a time when we played outside rather than staring at phone screens all day. Sports consumed most of my life which lead me to Kansas State University to play basketball.

I attended KSU for two year before feeling that my life was not heading in a good direction. I decided to go to the United States Navy in search of the structure and direction that my life was missing. In the Navy I was a Radiation Health Physics Technician for five year. Though the work was tough and the days were long, i spent the early part of my 20's traveling the world. This was a time that I would never replace.

Since my time in the Navy I have completed my undergraduates degree and continued on to obtain an MBA. Currently I work as a Marketing and Development Director for the Boy Scouts of America. Though this role has given me some great professional experiences, my true passing and skill set is with web development. To enhance my skills I have enrolled in Sothern Methodist University's Full Stack Web Development course.
